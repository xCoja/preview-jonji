XP Earning Rates

XP is earned based on the game's Return to Player (RTP) and bet type:

  • Sports Bets: 3 XP per $1 wagered (Min. odds 1.10)
  • 97% RTP & below: 1.5 XP per $1 wagered
  • 97% – 99% RTP: 1 XP per $1 wagered
  • Above 99% RTP: 0.5 XP per $1 wagered
Game Weighting

To keep the leaderboard fair, Slot games provide the most progress. High-RTP or "easy-to-grind" games like Blackjack and Plinko count significantly less toward your total.
